USAGE
  In order to use 'download_abide_preproc.py' you need to specify a derivative, a pipeline, and a strategy to search for. 
    [for complete toolkit ./data/abide-master/download_abide_preproc_guide.txt]
    
The following are the ones needed for this project:

DERIVATIVES
    - func_preproc (Preprocessed functional image)
    - lfcd (Local functional connectivity density)
    - reho (Regional homogeneity)
    - rois_aal (Timeseries extracted from the Automated Anatomical Labeling atlas)
    - rois_cc200 (" " from Cameron Craddock's 200 ROI parcellation atlas)
    - rois_cc400 (" " " 400 ROI parcellation atlas)
    - rois_dosenbach160 (" " from the Dosenbach160 atlas)
    - rois_ez (" " from the Eickhoff-Zilles atlas)
    - rois_ho (" " from the Harvard-Oxford atlas)
    - rois_tt (" " from the Talaraich and Tournoux atlas)
    - vmhc (Voxel-mirrored homotopic connectivity)

PIPELINE
  CCS: Center for Computational Sciences (CCS) is a software package developed at the University of Kentucky for analysis of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) data. 
  It provides a range of tools for processing and analyzing MRI data, including data conversion, image processing, and statistical analysis.
  
STRATEGIES
  -nofilt_noglobal (neither global signal regression nor band-pass filtering)
